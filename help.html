Help & Support

How do I lodge a complaint?

Use the 'Lodge a Complaint' feature on the homepage to submit your grievance with relevant details and attachments.

How can I track my complaint status?

Use your complaint ID or registered mobile number in the 'Track Complaint Status' section to get real-time updates.

What types of complaints can I file?

Complaints related to electricity, water, transportation, municipal services, and other local civic issues are accepted.
