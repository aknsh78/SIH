Local Area Complaint Management System

About the System

This complaint management system provides residents of our community an easy and efficient way to report local issues, such as utility problems, public infrastructure faults, sanitation concerns, and other civic matters.

Purpose

Our goal is to offer a transparent platform where complaints can be submitted, tracked, and resolved promptly to improve community well-being and trust in local governance.

Benefits for Residents

  • Quick and simple complaint registration.
  • Track the status of your complaints online.
  • Receive timely updates and reminders.
  • Ensure your concerns are heard and addressed by the right authorities.

How to Use

Register your complaint by choosing the appropriate category and filling out the form with relevant details. Once submitted, you can track your complaint status by providing your complaint ID or mobile number.
